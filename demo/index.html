Abstract

Diffusion and flow models make convincing sound for silent video, but they are slow — dozens of sequential steps, doubled again by guidance. FoleyDash rebuilds the training objective instead of the network: a forward-only α-Flow target with guidance folded in and a step-size-aware timestep. Trained from scratch on VGGSound, it matches a strong multi-step diffusion baseline using only four steps.
